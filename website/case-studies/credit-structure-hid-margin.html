When the credit structure quietly ate the margin

context

industry: Industrial components distribution

situation: Sponsor thesis centered on purchasing scale; working-capital swings were framed as “seasonality.”

capital involved

~$60M term + revolver basket (illustrative)

what happened

As rates moved, covenants forced behavior that looked rational quarter to quarter but degraded unit economics—small pricing concessions became structural.

outcome

Recoverable but slower path; realized return landed below the modeled base case (anonymized range; no identifiers).

insight

People model default risk first. In practice, behavioral risk under credit docs often dominates—especially when management is rewarded on volume, not on margin after true carrying cost.
