about Nomad

Nomad is built on experience from $300M+ in transactions and SMB financing, having helped businesses navigate capital and operations at PwC, Capgemini, Tiny Capital, and IQ. we’re launching our firm because we are builders who thrive in the arena, not on the sidelines.

we believe in deeply aligned incentives:

  • shared upside: we only win when our partners win.
  • mutual respect: we only work with people we like, admire, and respect.

after analyzing what great looks like in SMBs, we know most businesses don’t need more complexity. they need better capital allocation, smoother operations, and simple systems that actually work.

Nomad is also where we share how we think, what we’re learning, and how our views evolve as we build in public. if you own a great business and are thinking about scaling operations, or planning your next chapter, reach out.

views are my own. content is based on anonymized experiences and public information. no employer attribution. no identifiable transactions or client references.
