Edge is distribution, not novelty

Nov 2025

People want the story where a clever insight mints alpha. In the transactions I respect, alpha more often looks like access: who will actually pick up the phone, who trusts you with the messy truth, who will re‑up when the first version is embarrassing.

Novelty gets the headline; distribution gets the outcome. If you cannot trace a straight line from a thesis to a willing counterparty, you have a hobby.

Anonymized pattern: the best “strategic buyers” were rarely the highest multiple—they were the ones who could integrate without freezing the asset. The market rewarded operational fit more than narrative fit. The loudest auction rarely cleared cleanest.
